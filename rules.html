EFC Official Rules

Eligibility Requirements

Age

  • Must be 18+ to fight without parent involvement.
  • Fighters age 16–17 MUST have a parent or guardian present.
  • Valid ID is required before competing.

Waiver

  • All fighters MUST sign an official liability waiver before stepping into any match.
  • No waiver = no fight. No exceptions.

Condition

  • You must be physically capable of competing.
  • No visible injuries, intoxication, or impairment allowed.
  • Organizer has the right to deny any fighter for safety reasons.

Equipment Rules

Mandatory Gear

  • Mouthguard (required)
  • Groin protector (required)
  • Hand wraps (optional but recommended)
  • Gloves provided or approved by EFC

Clothing

  • No jewelry or metal of any kind.
  • Shorts only — no pockets, zippers, or chains.

Fight Conduct Rules

Illegal Strikes

  • No groin shots.
  • No strikes to the back of the head or spine.
  • No eye pokes.
  • No small joint manipulation (fingers/toes).
  • No headbutts.
  • No kicking or kneeing a grounded opponent in the head.

Legal Techniques

  • Boxing punches to legal areas.
  • Body shots.
  • Clinching allowed (limited).
  • Light grappling ONLY if agreed beforehand.

Stopping the Fight

  • The ref may stop at ANY time for safety.
  • Fighters may tap out verbally or physically.
  • If a fighter cannot intelligently defend, the match ends immediately.

Behavior Rules

Sportsmanship

  • No trash talk that crosses into harassment.
  • No fighting outside the designated match area.
  • Respect staff, refs, and fighters.

Automatic Disqualifications

  • Intentional fouls.
  • Continued illegal strikes after warnings.
  • Refusal to break when told.
  • Unsportsmanlike conduct.
  • Coming intoxicated or high.

Important Notice

All EFC fights are strictly exhibition matches. No fighter is to be considered a professional competitor. Signing the waiver means you accept full responsibility for injuries and risks. Failure to sign or follow rules results in instant removal from the event.
